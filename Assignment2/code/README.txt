-- Commands -- 
Begin Playing: Press Enter
Pause Original Song: Press Spacebar
Reset Song: Press Shift

Record Snippet: Press and hold ' for the duration of the snippet
Note: Recorded snippet will appear for playback at the next avaliable number key (0-9) which does not already hold a snippet. Up to 9 snippets can be recorded.

Play snippet: Press number keys. Snippets will continue looping unless their key is let go.

Shift snippets down a halfstep: press [
Shift snippets up a halfstep: press ]
